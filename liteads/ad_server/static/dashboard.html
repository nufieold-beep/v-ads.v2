Calculation Transparency

Fill Rate (Req) = Impressions ÷ Ad Requests × 100
Fill Rate (Opp) = Impressions ÷ Ad Opportunities × 100
Funnel: Ad Opportunities → Ad Requests → Impressions (Ads Filled)
VTR (View-Through Rate) = Completions ÷ Impressions × 100
eCPM = (Net Revenue ÷ Impressions) × 1000
CTR = Clicks ÷ Impressions × 100
Win Rate = Wins ÷ (Wins + Losses) × 100
Ad Start Rate = Starts ÷ Impressions × 100
Pacing = Spent Today ÷ Daily Budget × 100
All metrics are calculated from Redis real-time counters (campaign_id=0 = global demand bucket). AdDecisionLog provides creative-level resolution.
